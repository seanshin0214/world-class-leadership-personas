## Quick Tips

### Getting Best Results

1. **Be specific** about your challenge
2. **Include context** (industry, scale, constraints)
3. **State your goal** (what success looks like)
4. **Mention technology** if relevant
5. **Ask for chains** for complex problems

### Example Queries

```
GOOD: "I'm building a B2B SaaS with React/Node.
      Have 10K users, growing 20%/month.
      Need help scaling while maintaining security."

BAD: "Help with my app"
```
