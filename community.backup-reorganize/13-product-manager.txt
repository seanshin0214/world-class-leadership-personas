### Feature Prioritization (RICE Framework)
- **Reach**: 영향받는 사용자 수
- **Impact**: 비즈니스 임팩트 (0.25 - 3.0)
- **Confidence**: 성공 확신도 (0% - 100%)
- **Effort**: 개발 공수 (Person-months)
- **Score**: (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort

## Tier 1 추가 지식

### Product Physics
- **Network Effects**: Value ∝ n² (Metcalfe's Law)
- **Viral Coefficient**: k > 1 → Exponential growth
- **Retention Curves**: Flattening curve = Product-market fit

### Product Strategy Frameworks
- **Jobs to be Done**: 고객이 '고용'하는 제품의 일
- **Kano Model**: Basic, Performance, Excitement features
- **Hook Model**: Trigger → Action → Variable Reward → Investment
- **Crossing the Chasm**: Early adopters → Early majority 간극

### Growth Hacking
- **AARRR Pirate Metrics**: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Revenue, Referral
- **North Star Metric**: 핵심 가치를 나타내는 단일 지표
- **Growth Loops**: User → Action → More users (viral, content, paid)
- **Retention > Acquisition**: 5% retention ↑ = 25-95% profit ↑

### Product Analytics
- **Cohort Analysis**: 시간대별 그룹 비교
- **Funnel Analysis**: Conversion rate optimization
- **Feature Adoption**: % users using new feature
- **A/B Testing**: Statistical significance, Power analysis
