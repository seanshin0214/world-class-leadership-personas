## Copywriting Frameworks

```markdown
AIDA:

ATTENTION:
- Grab attention with headline
- "Stop Wasting Money on [Problem]"
- Bold claim, question, or curiosity gap

INTEREST:
- Build interest with benefits
- "Our platform helps you save 10 hours/week"
- Features â†’ Benefits transformation

DESIRE:
- Create desire with proof
- Social proof, testimonials, case studies
- "Join 10,000+ happy customers"

ACTION:
- Clear call-to-action
- "Start Your Free Trial"
- Remove friction, add urgency

---

PAS:

PROBLEM:
- Identify pain point
- "Tired of manual data entry?"
- Amplify frustration

AGITATE:
- Make problem worse
- "You're wasting 20 hours/month on repetitive tasks"
- Emotional impact

SOLUTION:
- Present your product as solution
- "Our automation saves you 20 hours/month"
- CTA: "Get Started Free"

---

BAB (Before-After-Bridge):

BEFORE:
- Current frustrating state
- "You're spending $10K/month on ads with poor ROI"

AFTER:
- Desired future state
- "Imagine cutting ad costs in half while doubling conversions"

BRIDGE:
- How to get there (your product)
- "Our AI optimizer achieves this automatically"
- CTA: "See How It Works"

---

FAB (Features-Advantages-Benefits):

FEATURE:
- What it is
- "24/7 customer support"

ADVANTAGE:
- What it does
- "Get help anytime, no waiting"

BENEFIT:
- What it means for customer
- "Sleep peacefully knowing we've got your back"
```

## Headlines

```markdown
HEADLINE FORMULAS:

NUMBER:
- "7 Ways to [Achieve Desired Outcome]"
- "10X Your [Metric] in 30 Days"
```

## Conversion Rate Optimization

```markdown
CRO PROCESS:

1. ANALYZE DATA:
   - Google Analytics (where users drop off)
   - Heatmaps (Hotjar, Crazy Egg)
   - Session recordings (see user behavior)
   - Identify bottlenecks

2. FORM HYPOTHESIS:
   - "If we simplify the form from 10 fields to 3, conversions will increase"
   - Based on data, not gut feeling

3. CREATE VARIATIONS:
   - Control (original)
   - Variation A (3-field form)
   - Variation B (3-field + progress bar)

4. RUN A/B TEST:
   - Split traffic 33/33/33
   - Run until statistical significance (95% confidence)
   - Need 1000+ conversions per variation

5. ANALYZE RESULTS:
   - Variation B increased conversions 25%!
   - Implement winner
   - Document learnings

6. ITERATE:
   - Continuous optimization
   - Test next hypothesis

---

COMMON TESTS:

HEADLINES:
- Different value propositions
- 20-50% lift common

CTA BUTTON:
- Color, text, size, placement
- 10-30% lift

FORM LENGTH:
- Fewer fields = higher conversion
- But may decrease lead quality (balance)

SOCIAL PROOF:
- Testimonials, reviews, logos
- 15-34% lift

IMAGES:
- Product screenshots vs lifestyle images
- Videos vs static images

PRICING:
- Displaying price vs hiding
- Annual vs monthly emphasis
- Tiered pricing (anchor effect)

---

TOOLS:
- Google Optimize (free A/B testing)
- Optimizely ($$$, enterprise)
- VWO (mid-market)
- Unbounce (landing page builder with A/B testing)
```

## Key Takeaways

1. **Benefit-driven** - Features tell, benefits sell
2. **Clarity > Cleverness** - Be clear, not cute
3. **Test everything** - A/B test headlines, CTAs, copy
4. **Remove friction** - Make it easy to say yes
5. **Keep it simple** - Short sentences, simple words
