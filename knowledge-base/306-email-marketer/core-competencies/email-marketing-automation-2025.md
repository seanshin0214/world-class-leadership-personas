## Email Strategy

```markdown
TYPES OF EMAILS:

PROMOTIONAL (Sales):
- Product launches
- Discounts, sales
- Limited-time offers
- Goal: Drive immediate purchases

TRANSACTIONAL:
- Order confirmation
- Shipping notification
- Password reset
- High open rates (80-90%)

NURTURE (Relationship):
- Weekly newsletter
- Educational content
- Company updates
- Goal: Build trust, engagement

TRIGGERED (Automated):
- Welcome series (after signup)
- Abandoned cart (left items in cart)
- Browse abandonment (viewed product, didn't add)
- Win-back (inactive subscribers)
- Goal: Timely, relevant

---

EMAIL FUNNEL:

AWARENESS (Top):
- Blog post roundup
- Industry news
- Free resources

CONSIDERATION (Middle):
- Case studies
- Product comparisons
- Webinar invitations

DECISION (Bottom):
- Free trial offer
- Product demo
- Discount code

LOYALTY (Post-purchase):
- Onboarding
- Tips & tricks
- Upsell, cross-sell
```

## List Building

```markdown
LEAD MAGNETS:

EBOOK/GUIDE:
- "Ultimate Guide to X"
- "10 Ways to Y"
- PDF, 10-20 pages

CHECKLIST:
- "SEO Audit Checklist"
- "Pre-Launch Checklist"
- 1-page PDF

TEMPLATE:
- "Email Template Library"
- "Social Media Calendar"
- Editable file (Google Sheets, Notion)

WEBINAR:
- Live training
- Q&A session
- Recording sent after

DISCOUNT:
- "10% off first purchase"
- "Free shipping"
- Code: WELCOME10

QUIZ:
- "What's Your Marketing Personality?"
- "Find Your Perfect Product"
- Interactive, personalized result

---

OPT-IN FORMS:

POPUP:
- Exit-intent (shows when leaving site)
- Timed (after 30 seconds)
- Scroll-triggered (after 50% page scroll)

INLINE:
- Embedded in blog post
- Sidebar widget
- Footer

LANDING PAGE:
- Dedicated page (no distractions)
- Single CTA (subscribe)
- Example: yoursite.com/free-guide

---

LIST HYGIENE:

DOUBLE OPT-IN:
- Subscriber confirms email
- Reduces fake emails, spam complaints
- Lower list size, but higher engagement

CLEAN LIST:
- Remove bounces (invalid emails)
- Remove inactive (no opens in 6+ months)
- Re-engagement campaign first ("We miss you!")

SEGMENTATION:
- By behavior (opened X, clicked Y)
- By purchase history
- By demographics
- By engagement level
```

## Automation Workflows

```markdown
WELCOME SERIES (New Subscriber):

EMAIL 1 (Immediate):
Subject: "Welcome to [Brand]! Here's your gift ğŸ"
Content:
- Thank you for subscribing
- Deliver lead magnet (ebook, discount)
- Introduce brand (what we do)
- CTA: "Shop Now" or "Read Blog"

EMAIL 2 (Day 3):
Subject: "Here's how to get started"
Content:
- Helpful tips
- Popular products/posts
- Customer testimonials
- CTA: "Explore"

EMAIL 3 (Day 7):
Subject: "Questions? We're here to help"
Content:
- FAQ
- Customer support info
- Invite to reply with questions
- CTA: "Contact Us"

EMAIL 4 (Day 14):
Subject: "Last chance: Your 10% discount expires soon"
Content:
- Reminder of discount
- Product showcase
- Urgency (expires in 48 hours)
- CTA: "Use My Discount"

---

ABANDONED CART (Left items in cart):

TRIGGER: Cart abandoned for 1 hour (no purchase)

EMAIL 1 (1 hour later):
Subject: "Did you forget something?"
Content:
- Show cart items
- "Complete your purchase"
- CTA: "Return to Cart"

EMAIL 2 (24 hours later):
Subject: "[Name], your cart is about to expire"
Content:
- Cart items
- Social proof ("200 customers bought this today")
- Urgency ("Only 3 left in stock")
- CTA: "Checkout Now"

EMAIL 3 (48 hours later):
Subject: "Last chance + 10% off your order"
Content:
- Cart items
- Discount code (sweetener)
- Testimonials
- CTA: "Use Code: SAVE10"

RECOVERY RATE:
- Email 1: 8-10%
- Email 2: 5-7%
- Email 3 (with discount): 10-15%
- Total: 20-30% recovery

---

WIN-BACK (Inactive Subscribers):

TRIGGER: No opens in 90 days

EMAIL 1:
Subject: "We miss you! ğŸ˜¢"
Content:
- "It's been a while..."
- "What would you like to see from us?"
- Survey (improve content)
- Discount ("Come back with 20% off")
- CTA: "Reactivate"

EMAIL 2 (7 days later, if no opens):
Subject: "Last email from us"
Content:
- "We don't want to clutter your inbox"
- "Click here to stay subscribed"
- "Or we'll remove you in 7 days"
- CTA: "Keep Me Subscribed"
```

## Metrics & Optimization

```markdown
KEY METRICS:

OPEN RATE:
- Formula: Opens / Delivered Ã— 100
- Good: >20%
- Improve: Better subject lines, send time, preheader

CLICK-THROUGH RATE (CTR):
- Formula: Clicks / Delivered Ã— 100
- Good: >2.5%
- Improve: Better CTA, relevant content, personalization

CONVERSION RATE:
- Formula: Conversions / Delivered Ã— 100
- Good: Varies by industry
- Ecommerce: 1-3%
- SaaS: 0.5-1%

BOUNCE RATE:
- Hard bounce: Invalid email (remove immediately)
- Soft bounce: Temporary (full inbox, server down)
- Good: <2%

UNSUBSCRIBE RATE:
- Good: <0.5%
- Red flag: >1% (content irrelevant, too frequent)

LIST GROWTH RATE:
- Formula: (New Subscribers - Unsubscribes) / Total Subscribers Ã— 100
- Good: 5-10% per month

REVENUE PER EMAIL:
- Formula: Total Revenue / Emails Sent
- Benchmark: $0.10-$1.00 (varies widely)

---

A/B TESTING:

WHAT TO TEST:
- Subject line (biggest impact)
- Send time (morning vs evening, weekday vs weekend)
- CTA (button text, color, placement)
- Layout (image vs text-heavy)
- Personalization (with vs without name)

METHOD:
1. Split list (50/50 or 10/10/80)
2. Change ONE variable
3. Send both versions
4. Wait 2-4 hours
5. Analyze winner
6. Send winner to rest (if using 10/10/80 method)

SIGNIFICANCE:
- Need at least 1000 subscribers per variation
- Aim for 95% confidence level
- Tools: A/B Test Significance Calculator

---

SEND TIME OPTIMIZATION:

BEST TIMES (General):
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 10 AM - 11 AM (morning coffee)
- 2 PM - 3 PM (post-lunch)

BUT:
- Test YOUR audience
- B2B: Weekday mornings
- B2C: Evenings, weekends
- Varies by time zone, industry

FREQUENCY:
- Too little: Forgotten
- Too much: Unsubscribes
- Sweet spot: 1-4Ã— per week (test!)
```

## Deliverability

```markdown
SENDER REPUTATION:

SPF (Sender Policy Framework):
- DNS record authorizes servers to send email
- Prevents spoofing
- Set up in DNS: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all

DKIM (DomainKeys Identified Mail):
- Digital signature verifies email
- Prevents tampering
- Email provider generates keys

DMARC (Domain-based Message Authentication):
- Policy for SPF/DKIM failures
- Tells receiving server what to do (quarantine, reject)
- Set up: v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:dmarc@yourdomain.com

---

AVOID SPAM FILTERS:

CONTENT:
- Avoid spam words (FREE, ACT NOW, GUARANTEE)
- Balance text/image ratio (60/40)
- No ALL CAPS
- Include unsubscribe link (required by law!)

ENGAGEMENT:
- High open/click rates = Good sender
- Low engagement = Spam folder
- Clean list regularly

IP WARM-UP (New domain):
- Start small (100 emails/day)
- Gradually increase (double every few days)
- Full send (50K+) after 2-4 weeks

---

LEGAL COMPLIANCE:

CAN-SPAM ACT (US):
- Unsubscribe link (must honor within 10 days)
- Physical address (in footer)
- No deceptive subject lines
- Penalty: $46,517 per violation

GDPR (EU):
- Explicit consent (no pre-checked boxes)
- Right to access data
- Right to delete data
- Privacy policy
- Penalty: â‚¬20 million or 4% of revenue
```

## Key Takeaways

1. **Permission-based** - Only email those who opted in
2. **Mobile-first** - 50%+ opens on mobile (design accordingly)
3. **Automation** - Set up workflows (saves time, increases revenue)
4. **Testing** - A/B test subject lines, send times, content
5. **Clean list** - Remove inactive, improves deliverability
