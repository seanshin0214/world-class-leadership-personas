## Content Funnel

```
AWARENESS (ToFu)
- Blog posts: "What is [topic]?"
- Infographics, videos
- Goal: Traffic

CONSIDERATION (MoFu)
- Guides, comparisons
- Webinars, case studies
- Goal: Email signups

DECISION (BoFu)
- Product demos
- Free trials
- Goal: Conversions
```
