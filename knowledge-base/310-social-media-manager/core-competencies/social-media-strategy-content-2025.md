## Platform Strategies

```markdown
INSTAGRAM (Visual, lifestyle, younger audience):

DEMOGRAPHICS:
- 67% ages 18-29
- 47% ages 30-49
- Female-skewed (but balanced)

CONTENT TYPES:
- Feed posts (photos, carousels): Polished, curated
- Stories (24 hours): Behind-the-scenes, casual, polls
- Reels (<90 sec video): Trending audio, entertainment, education
- IGTV (long-form video): Deprecated, use Reels
- Live: Q&A, product launches, events

BEST PRACTICES:
- Post frequency: 3-7Ã— per week (feed), daily (stories)
- Optimal times: Lunch (11am-1pm), evening (7-9pm) weekdays
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant (max 30, but spammy)
  * Mix: Branded (#YourBrand), niche (#VeganRecipes), broad (#Foodie)
- Captions: Hook first line (pre "more"), call-to-action (CTA)
- Engagement: Respond to comments within 1 hour (algorithm boost)

ALGORITHM:
- Prioritizes: Engagement (saves > shares > comments > likes)
- Recency: Post when audience online (check Insights)
- Relationships: Accounts user interacts with most
- Reels: Pushed to Explore (discoverability)

---

TIKTOK (Short video, Gen Z, trending):

DEMOGRAPHICS:
- 60% ages 16-24
- 26% ages 25-44
- Global reach

CONTENT TYPES:
- Videos: 15 sec - 10 min (sweet spot: 21-34 sec)
- Trends: Challenges, dances, sounds (jump on fast, <48 hours)
- Duets/Stitches: Collaborate, react to others

BEST PRACTICES:
- Post frequency: 1-4Ã— daily (consistency matters)
- Hook: First 3 seconds critical (grab attention)
- Trending sounds: Use popular audio (discoverability)
- Text overlays: Most watch without sound
- Hashtags: 3-5, mix trending + niche
- Native editing: Film in-app (algorithm favors over uploads)

ALGORITHM (For You Page):
- User interactions (likes, shares, watch time, rewatches)
- Video info (captions, sounds, hashtags)
- Device/account settings (language, location)
- NOT follower count (anyone can go viral)

---

LINKEDIN (Professional, B2B, thought leadership):

DEMOGRAPHICS:
- 60% ages 25-34
- 57% male, 43% female
- High-income, college-educated

CONTENT TYPES:
- Text posts: Insights, lessons, stories
- Articles: Long-form (1000+ words)
- Videos: Talking head, interviews, tips
- Carousels (PDFs): Swipeable, educational

BEST PRACTICES:
- Post frequency: 2-5Ã— per week (quality > quantity)
- Optimal times: Tue-Thu, 8-10am, 12pm (work hours)
- Tone: Professional but authentic (no corporate speak)
- Engagement bait: Ask questions, polls, tag people
- First comment: Add link (posts with links in caption get suppressed)

ALGORITHM:
- Dwell time (how long people read)
- Engagement rate (especially from connections)
- Relevance (to your network's interests)

---

TWITTER/X (News, real-time, conversation):

DEMOGRAPHICS:
- 38% ages 18-29
- 26% ages 30-49
- Male-skewed

CONTENT TYPES:
- Tweets (280 characters): Thoughts, news, hot takes
- Threads: Multiple tweets (tell story, listicle)
- Replies: Join conversations
- Spaces: Audio rooms (live discussion)

BEST PRACTICES:
- Post frequency: 3-10Ã— daily (high volume platform)
- Optimal times: Weekdays 8am, 12pm, 5pm
- Brevity: Shorter tweets (100-150 char) get more engagement
- Hashtags: 1-2 max (overuse looks desperate)
- Visuals: Tweets with images get 150% more retweets
- Engage: Reply to others (build network)

ALGORITHM:
- Engagement (retweets, likes, replies)
- Recency (real-time feed)
- Blue checkmark (boost for paid subscribers)

---

YOUTUBE (Long-form video, search, evergreen):

DEMOGRAPHICS:
- 81% of US adults use
- All age groups (broad reach)

CONTENT TYPES:
- Long-form (8+ min): Tutorials, vlogs, reviews
- Shorts (<60 sec): TikTok-style, vertical
- Livestreams: Gaming, Q&A, events

BEST PRACTICES:
- Post frequency: 1-3Ã— per week (consistency)
- Title: Keyword-rich, curiosity ("How I Made $10K in 30 Days")
- Thumbnail: High-contrast, text, faces (emotion)
- Description: First 2 lines visible (hook), keywords, links
- Tags: 5-8 relevant keywords
- CTAs: Subscribe, like, comment (in video + description)
- Watch time: Algorithm favors (8+ min videos can have mid-roll ads)

ALGORITHM:
- Click-through rate (CTR): Thumbnail + title
- Watch time: % of video watched (avg 50% = good)
- Engagement: Likes, comments, shares
- Session time: Do viewers watch more YouTube after your video?

---

FACEBOOK (Broad audience, groups, video):

DEMOGRAPHICS:
- 77% ages 30-49
- 73% ages 50-64
- Older than other platforms

CONTENT TYPES:
- Posts (text, images, links)
- Videos (native upload > YouTube links)
- Stories (24 hours)
- Groups: Build community
- Live: Events, Q&A

BEST PRACTICES:
- Post frequency: 1-2Ã— daily
- Optimal times: Weekdays 1-4pm
- Engagement bait: Questions, fill-in-the-blank, tag a friend
- Native video: Upload directly (Facebook prioritizes over YouTube)
- Groups: More reach than Pages (algorithm shift)

ALGORITHM:
- Meaningful interactions (comments > reacts > shares > views)
- Friends/family prioritized over brands (Pages struggle)
- Video watch time
- Groups favored (join/create relevant groups)
```

## Content Creation

```markdown
CONTENT PILLARS (3-5 themes):

EXAMPLE (Fitness brand):
1. Education (workout tips, nutrition)
2. Inspiration (transformation stories, motivation quotes)
3. Behind-the-scenes (gym life, trainer intros)
4. Community (user-generated content, shoutouts)
5. Promotion (products, services, sales)

RATIO: 80/20 rule
- 80% value (educate, entertain, inspire)
- 20% promotion (sell)

- Film 4-8 videos in one session (same outfit, different topics)
- Write 10-20 captions in advance
```

---

## Analytics & Optimization

```markdown
KEY METRICS:

REACH:
- Impressions (total views)
- Reach (unique viewers)
- Follower growth (net new followers)

ENGAGEMENT:
- Engagement rate = (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers Ã— 100%
  * 1-3% = Average
  * 3-6% = Good
  * 6%+ = Excellent
- Save rate (Instagram): Indicates value
- Share rate: Indicates resonance

CONVERSION:
- Click-through rate (CTR): Link clicks / impressions
- Conversion rate: Purchases / link clicks
- Cost per acquisition (CPA): Ad spend / conversions

---

PLATFORM ANALYTICS:

INSTAGRAM INSIGHTS (Business account):
- Accounts reached (how many saw your content)
- Accounts engaged (interacted)
- Top posts (by reach, engagement)
- Follower demographics (age, gender, location, active times)

TIKTOK ANALYTICS:
- Video views
- Profile views
- Follower growth
- Trending videos
- Traffic source (For You vs Following vs Hashtag)

YOUTUBE ANALYTICS:
- Watch time (hours)
- Average view duration (how much of video watched)
- Click-through rate (thumbnail impressions â†’ clicks)
- Traffic sources (YouTube search, suggested, external)

---

A/B TESTING:

TEST VARIABLES:
- Posting time (morning vs evening)
- Caption length (short vs long)
- Hashtags (3 vs 10 vs 30)
- Content type (photo vs video vs carousel)
- Thumbnail (face vs text vs product)

METHOD:
1. Change ONE variable at a time
2. Post 2 versions (A vs B)
3. Measure (engagement rate, reach)
4. Repeat winner

EXAMPLE:
- Post 1: Caption 50 words, 5 hashtags â†’ 2.5% engagement
- Post 2: Caption 50 words, 20 hashtags â†’ 1.8% engagement
- Conclusion: Fewer hashtags better (for your account)

---

REPORTING:

MONTHLY REPORT TEMPLATE:
- Summary (key wins, challenges)
- Growth (followers, engagement rate)
- Top posts (3 best-performing)
- Audience insights (demographics, active times)
- Recommendations (what to do next month)

TOOLS:
- Native analytics (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
- Google Analytics (website traffic from social)
- Sprout Social, Hootsuite (cross-platform reporting)
```

## Community Management

```markdown
RESPONDING TO COMMENTS:

TIMING:
- First hour critical (algorithm boost)
- Aim: Respond to all comments within 24 hours

TONE:
- Friendly, human (not robotic)
- Use emojis (match brand voice)
- Answer questions (helpful)
- Thank for positive feedback

EXAMPLES:
- "Love this!" â†’ "Thank you! ðŸ˜Š"
- "Where did you get that shirt?" â†’ "It's from [Brand], link in bio!"
- "This doesn't work" â†’ "Sorry to hear! Can you DM us details so we can help?"

---

HANDLING NEGATIVE COMMENTS:

RESPOND PUBLICLY (Show you care):
- "Thanks for the feedback. We're sorry you had this experience. Please DM us so we can make it right."

MOVE TO DM (Resolve privately):
- Avoid public argument
- Gather details, offer solution
- Follow up publicly ("Glad we could resolve this!")

DELETE/BLOCK (Rare):
- Spam (bots, scams)
- Hate speech, harassment
- Competitors (posting their links)
- Excessive profanity

---

ENGAGEMENT TACTICS:

ASK QUESTIONS:
- "What's your favorite...?"
- "A or B?"
- "Fill in the blank: I wish I could..."

POLLS (Instagram Stories):
- Quick, easy interaction
- Binary choice (This or That?)

TAG PROMPTS:
- "Tag someone who..."
- "Tag your partner in crime"
- (Don't overuse, feels spammy)

RESPOND TO DMs:
- Personalized (use their name)
- Quick replies (saved responses for FAQs)
- Don't ghost (builds loyalty)

---

INFLUENCER COLLABORATION:

TYPES:
- Nano (1K-10K followers): High engagement, affordable ($100-$500/post)
- Micro (10K-100K): Niche, trusted ($500-$5K)
- Macro (100K-1M): Broad reach ($5K-$50K+)
- Mega (1M+): Celebrity status ($50K-$500K+)

FINDING:
- Hashtags (search your niche)
- Influencer platforms (AspireIQ, Upfluence)
- Engage first (comment, DM, build relationship)

OUTREACH:
- Personalized (don't copy-paste)
- Value prop ("We love your content on X, want to collab?")
- Clear terms (deliverables, compensation, timeline)

EXAMPLE EMAIL:
> Hi [Name],
> 
> I love your content on [topic]! Your recent post on [specific post] really resonated with our brand.
> 
> We'd love to partner with you for [campaign]. We're offering [compensation: $500 or free product] for [deliverables: 1 feed post + 3 stories].
> 
> Interested? Let's chat!
> 
> [Your name]

---

CRISIS MANAGEMENT:

TYPES OF CRISES:
- Product issue (defect, recall)
- PR misstep (offensive post, controversy)
- Security breach (hacked account)
- Negative viral post (complaint goes viral)

RESPONSE PROTOCOL:
1. PAUSE posting (don't schedule auto-posts during crisis)
2. ASSESS situation (What happened? Who's affected?)
3. RESPOND quickly (within hours, not days)
   - Acknowledge issue
   - Take responsibility (if at fault)
   - Explain action plan ("We're investigating and will update within 24 hours")
4. MONITOR (track mentions, sentiment)
5. FOLLOW UP (resolution, lessons learned)

EXAMPLE:
> We're aware of the issue with [product]. We sincerely apologize and are working urgently to resolve this. All affected customers will receive [refund/replacement]. Updates to follow.

DON'T:
- Delete comments (Streisand effect, looks like cover-up)
- Argue with customers
- Go silent (silence = guilt)
```
